Overview

The history of PicoContaner is tied to that of IoC itself. There are three main types of Inversion of Control.  The names for these have been recently evolved by Martin Fowler and friends and publicised here.
  1. Contextualized Dependency Lookup
  2. Setter Dependency Injector
  3. Constructor Dependency Injector
One, two and three were roughly evolved in that order.  One has been diminant for a number of years.  PicoContainer authors have either formerly promoted it, or avoided it because it felt overly complicated.

Two and three came into being within a short space of each other in the last year or so.  Both were created as part of aquest for transparency and simplicity.
